iNfoAdPlatform

The iNfoAdPlatform product solves the problem of managing advertisement and data content for small business and the general public in a very cost effective manner using wireless/mobile technology.

How It Works?

A store would have one or multiple displays with the iNfoAdPlatform wireless STB attached. A video stream will be sent to the display with a combination of news, stories, and advertising. A customer can customize the video stream as desired and each customer has a unique stream, or the customer can just buy commercial time in the stream that serves multiple customers.

Advantage

The solution will provide streaming video to static content over the 3G wireless network and also will be backed by the high performance ad network servers across the nation. The product is designed in such a fashion that even a customer with a single store can subscribe to one set top box or device and also scalable to retailers with even 10,000 stores or more.
